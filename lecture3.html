Executives

Head of state vs. head of government

Presidential, parliamentary, and semi-presidential regimes.

The role of ministers and cabinets

  • Responsible for area of government.
  • Head of bureaucracy.
  • Prepare and develop legislation.
  • Advisors to chief executive.

Group exercise

In groups or 2-3 people discuss the following questions:

In Germany and/or your home country...

  • Is this a presidential, parliamentary, or semi-presidential system?
  • How is the head of state chosen?
  • How is the head of government chosen?
  • Are there fixed terms for head of state/governement?
  • Who selects ministers for governement/cabinet?
  • Are single-party or coalition governments more common?
  • Do governements tend to have a majority in parliament?
  • How well would you say the governement works in this country in terms of efficiency, addressing relevant issues, and not abusing it's powers?
  • Why do you think this works well/not so well in this particular system?

Legislatives

  • Roles of legislatives: Make laws, oversee executives, and represent constituencies.
  • Descriptive representation vs. substantive representation.
  • Delegates vs. trustees.
  • Duverger's Law
  • Executive dominance - a threat to democracy?

Group exercise

In groups or 2-3 people discuss the following questions:

In Germany and/or your home country...

  • Uni-cameral or bi-cameral? If bi-cameral how are the two related?
  • Single or multi-member constituencies?
  • Which tools does the leglislature have to oversee the government and potentially keep the executive accountable?
  • Which tools does the legislature have to represent constituencies?
  • Does the legislature have resources to prepare legislation? Which?
  • Does the legislative have any tools or resources to review legislation?
  • Who sets the agenda in parliament?
  • How well would you say this legislature works in terms of efficiency, addressing relevant issues, and overseeing the governement?
  • Why do you think this works well/not so well in this particular system?
